Research

Our lab develops immune-competent guinea-pig models to study human virus-associated cancers, with a focus on Kaposi’s sarcoma-associated herpesvirus (KSHV) and Epstein–Barr virus (EBV). We integrate primary cell culture, in vivo models, single-cell genomics, and translational pharmacology.

Focus areas

  • Innate sensing and IRF7/MDA5 pathways during early infection
  • Endothelial cell infection and tumorigenesis
  • Preclinical testing of antivirals, vaccines, and epigenetic modulators
