Planning a Visit?

🚗 Parking

Visitor parking available in designated areas on campus. Daily parking passes can be purchased at campus entrances or through the LSU parking app.

🏢 Building Access

The School of Veterinary Medicine requires ID for entry. Please coordinate your visit in advance for building access and lab tours.

🚌 Transportation

LSU is accessible via Capital Area Transit (CATS) bus system. The campus is also walkable and bike-friendly with rental options available.

🏨 Accommodations

Baton Rouge offers various lodging options from budget to luxury. The Cook Hotel on LSU campus provides convenient on-site accommodation.

⏰ Office Hours

Lab meetings and office visits available Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM. Please schedule in advance to ensure availability.

📋 Lab Safety

All lab visitors must complete safety training and wear appropriate PPE. We'll provide detailed safety protocols before your visit.
